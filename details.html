Fees

The standard summer school registration fee this year is 1400 Euro. Payment can be made either by bank transfer or credit card. This fee pays for:

  • Accommodation for 11 nights in a room shared between two participants. Room allocation based on gender.
  • Dinner for 11 nights.
  • Lunch and coffee breaks for the 9 school days (no catering available at school on Sunday, although the venue will still be open for the enthusiastic).
  • Attendance at the school itself.

Single-room option: For the above fee, rooms are shared between pairs of participants. To guarantee a single room, a total fee of 1800 Euro is required.

Reduced-rate option: For those living nearby, or those who wish to manage their own accommodation, a reduced rate may be available (but is not guaranteed at this point).
